ABOUT

This project was created as a part of the course Information Visualizaton at KTH. Data is retrieved from LinkedIn, Wikipedia and KTH Alumni.

PURPOSE

Engineer Your Future enables current and future students at KTH to explore opportunities based on their education. The goal is to give students a vision of what their education can bring whether it be career, profession or skills.
